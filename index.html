The Grassroots Internet Project

A Queer Anarchist's Internet

The internet connects us at all times, whether we like it or otherwise. But it is increasingly becoming a place where we are constantly surveiled, barely able to see a friend's lastest gig photos without the bombardments of adverts and cookies.

The Grassroots Internet Project is a simple one, aiming to bring back simple websites, blogs, and a move away from gargantuan media platforms for organisations in Manchester. It is a collection of templates and tutorials on how to create your own static websites, free from cookies, censorship, or subscriptions. It is also a homepage for these sites, linking to sites that wish to join the project.

The site also acts as an index of unaffiliated organisations in and around manchester, and may link to pages and accounts outside of the project.
