Making a One-Page Site

A simple way to host your links

A one-page site refers to a website that usually has a simple list of links or images on a single page. Typically, these sites are hosted by a platform that allows users to easily create these pages. If you want to make a one-page site with html yourself, follow the tutorial on creating a website from scratch.

The most common of these hosts are Campsite, Carrd, and Linktree. While all three are fairly similar, Carrd allows more customisation than just listing links and setting a theme, acting closer to a simple website builder. However, Linktree has a mobile app that may make it more accessible to use.

Campsite

Best when you want a list of links, but with some design customisation available beyond just themes.

Carrd

Best when you want something beyond a list of links, with customisation options that allow you to get close to a multi-page site.

Linktree

Best when you want a list of links without too much focus on customisation, but with a mobile app to be better able to create your page.

Once you've chosen the site you want to use, follow the guide it gives you and you'll soon have a basic site with all your links that you can add to!
